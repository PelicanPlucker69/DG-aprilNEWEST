ABOUT OUR PRACTICE

Philosophy

We recognise and pay respect to the Elders and communities – past, present, and emerging – of traditional lands of the Wallumedegal people of the Eora Nation. “Always was, Always will be.”

At Dental Garden, we focus on providing a serene and caring environment for our patients, ensuring that every visit is comfortable and stress-free. Our philosophy emphasises tranquillity, comprehensive and gentle dental care, and clinical excellence. We are dedicated to eco-friendly practices, maintaining high standards of integrity, and using evidence-based dentistry to stay current with the latest advancements in dental science. By combining these values, we aim to offer the best possible dental care experience. Welcome to Dental Garden, where your smile is our priority.

Community

At Dental Garden, we believe in giving back to our community and making a positive impact. We are proud of our altruistic approach to dentistry. Our team, led by Dr Frances Ershad, donates their time to the Filling the Gap charity, providing pro bono dental treatment to those in need. We are also proud to support local organizations. Dental Garden has donated vouchers for charity auctions at Boronia Park Public School and the Gladesville Ravens Women’s Football Team. We are dedicated to supporting local education and fostering healthy lifestyles within our community. At Dental Garden, we serve with care, integrity, and excellence. Together, we aren't just creating smiles, but making a meaningful difference in the lives of those around us.

Technology

At Dental Garden, we use advanced technology to improve patient care and comfort. Our tools include Cone Beam CT and OPG, which provide detailed 3D images of teeth and bones for precise diagnoses, and digital radiography, offering X-rays with up to 90% less radiation than traditional ones, providing clear, instant images. We also utilise digital photography to capture high-quality images of teeth and gums to track progress and share results. Our CEREC system allows us to create custom crowns in a single visit with 3D scanning and milling. For added comfort, we have installed televisions on the ceilings of our treatment rooms, so you can enjoy your favourite shows during treatments. We believe that investing in technology enhances our patients' health and comfort at Dental Garden.

Children

At Dental Garden, we create a fun, child-friendly environment to ensure our youngest patients feel seen, heard, and cared for. Our treatment rooms have ceiling-mounted TVs, allowing kids to watch their favourite cartoons during visits, helping them feel relaxed. Each child receives a toy to take home, building positive associations with dental visits. We offer various flavours for treatments and provide sensory toys for anxious children. To accommodate busy schedules, we open at 8:00 AM and offer weekend appointments. Our goal is to provide excellent dental care and form positive experiences for children that last a lifetime.

Anxious Patients

We aim to make dental visits comfortable and stress-free, using cognitive calming techniques to minimise anxiety. Our dedicated team tailors care to your needs, ensuring a positive experience without sedation or general anesthesia. Explore our website and videos to learn more, and find additional resources on dental anxiety from the Australian Dental Association and Beyond Blue. Seniors

At Dental Garden, we prioritize the unique dental needs of our senior patients by offering comprehensive and compassionate care. To show our appreciation, we provide a 10% discount on all dental services for seniors and pensioners. Our treatments include safe and comfortable tooth extractions, various options for replacing missing teeth such as dentures, bridges, and dental implants, and comprehensive periodontal care to maintain gum health. We are committed to delivering the highest standard of care in a welcoming environment, helping seniors maintain healthy smiles at every stage of life.
